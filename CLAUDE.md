# Network School Robot

An AI assistant for the Network School community that serves as a personal coach, knowledge base, and daily companion.

## Project Purpose

A conversational bot that helps Network School members with:

- **Life & Business Coaching** - Goal setting, accountability, feedback on ideas and plans
- **Network School Knowledge** - Information about NS programs, culture, and resources
- **Logistics** - Practical questions about living/working at Network School
- **Luma Calendar** - Event discovery and scheduling assistance
- **Discord Integration** - Context from community conversations
- **Wiki Access** - Quick access to Network School wiki content
- **Daily Check-ins** - Build a relationship over time, remembering user context and progress

## Core Philosophy

- Be a supportive friend that remembers you
- Encourage daily check-ins to build continuity
- Provide honest, constructive feedback
- Help members get the most out of their Network School experience
